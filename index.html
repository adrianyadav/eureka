Supposedly 'Eureka' means 'I have found it' and dont worry fair patrons, you have found it! With a mixture of personalities among our staff to make each wary traveler's experience a comfortable, unpretentious one might ask 'how can such a place exist?' The answer is, it just does!

So fine person who stumbled through our doors with glee or anticiaption, thank you for reading this far and welcome. Take a look around, make yourself known and explore our menus. Drool over our craft beers and wine, and relax.
